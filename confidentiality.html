Confidentiality

Jd AI is committed to keeping your conversations and personal data private and protected.

1. Secure Access

Access is managed through Firebase Authentication. Only authenticated users can access your account and stored chats.

2. Data Storage

Conversations are stored in Firebase Firestore under a user-specific document structure. Data is not visible to other users unless explicitly shared.

3. Confidentiality Duration

Your data remains confidential indefinitely unless you delete your account or messages.

4. Request Removal

To remove your data, please contact support or use the provided account controls.
