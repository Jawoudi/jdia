Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This page explains how Jd AI collects and uses data.

1. Information Collection

We collect authentication data (email and user ID) from Firebase Auth to identify your account and save your conversations securely in Firestore.

2. Data Usage

Data is used to store your conversations, personalize your experience, and restore session history across devices. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Security

We rely on Firebase security protocols, including encrypted transport (HTTPS) and server-side security rules, plus user-based access restrictions.

4. Contact

If you have questions, please contact us using the support link in the app or via email (your support email).
